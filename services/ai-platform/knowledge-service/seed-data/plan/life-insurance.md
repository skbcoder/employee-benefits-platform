# Life Insurance and AD&D Plans

## Overview

The company provides basic life insurance and accidental death & dismemberment (AD&D) coverage at no cost to all eligible employees. Employees may also purchase supplemental life insurance and dependent life insurance at group rates.

## Basic Life Insurance (Employer-Paid)

All eligible employees automatically receive basic life insurance at no cost:

- Coverage Amount: 1x annual base salary (rounded up to the next $1,000)
- Maximum Coverage: $200,000
- AD&D Coverage: Equal to life insurance amount
- Beneficiary: Designated by employee (default: estate if no beneficiary on file)
- Portability: May be converted to an individual policy upon termination
- Effective Date: Same as other benefits (first of month after 30 days of employment)

## Supplemental Life Insurance (Employee-Paid)

Employees may purchase additional life insurance coverage:

### Employee Supplemental Life

- Coverage Options: 1x, 2x, 3x, 4x, or 5x annual base salary
- Maximum Coverage: $500,000
- Guaranteed Issue: Up to 3x salary for new hires enrolling within 30 days (no medical underwriting required)
- Evidence of Insurability: Required for amounts exceeding guaranteed issue, late enrollees, and coverage increases outside of initial eligibility
- Monthly Premium Rates (per $1,000 of coverage):

### Supplemental AD&D (Employee)

- Coverage Options: $25,000 increments up to $500,000
- Guaranteed Issue: Full amount for new hires
- Monthly Premium: $0.02 per $1,000 of coverage (all ages)
- Covers: Accidental death, loss of limb, loss of sight, loss of hearing, paralysis

## Dependent Life Insurance

Employees may purchase life insurance for their dependents:

### Spouse Life Insurance
- Coverage Options: $10,000, $25,000, $50,000, $75,000, or $100,000
- Guaranteed Issue: $25,000 for new hires enrolling within 30 days
- Evidence of Insurability: Required for amounts over $25,000
- Monthly Premium: Based on employee's age band (same rates as supplemental)

### Child Life Insurance
- Coverage Amount: $5,000 or $10,000 per child
- Covers: All eligible dependent children (14 days to age 26)
- Monthly Premium: $1.50 ($5,000 coverage) or $3.00 ($10,000 coverage) for all children combined
- No medical underwriting required

## Accelerated Death Benefit

If an employee covered under the basic or supplemental life insurance plan is diagnosed with a terminal illness (life expectancy of 12 months or less), they may receive an accelerated payment of up to 75% of their life insurance benefit. The remaining 25% is paid to the beneficiary upon death.

## Beneficiary Designation

Employees must designate a primary and contingent beneficiary for their life insurance coverage. Beneficiary designations can be updated at any time through the benefits enrollment system. It is recommended that employees review and update their beneficiary designations annually and after any qualifying life event.

## Imputed Income

Under IRS rules, employer-paid group term life insurance coverage exceeding $50,000 results in imputed income to the employee. The imputed income amount is based on IRS Table I rates and is reported on the employee's W-2. Employees should consult a tax advisor for questions about imputed income.

## Portability and Conversion

Upon termination of employment, employees may:
- Port their supplemental life insurance (continue coverage at group rates for up to 12 months)
- Convert to an individual whole life policy (without evidence of insurability, within 31 days of termination)

Ported and converted policies are at the employee's full expense with no employer contribution.

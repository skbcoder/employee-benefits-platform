# Vision Plan Options

## Overview

The company offers two vision plan options through VSP (Vision Service Plan). Both plans cover annual eye exams and provide allowances for corrective lenses or contact lenses.

## Plan Comparison

### Basic Vision Plan

- Monthly Premium: $8 (employee only), $16 (employee + spouse), $14 (employee + children), $22 (family)
- Eye Exam: $10 copay, 1 per year
- Prescription Lenses: $25 copay, 1 pair per year
- Frames Allowance: $130 per year (in-network retail)
- Contact Lens Allowance: $130 per year (in lieu of frames/lenses)
- Contact Lens Fitting: Up to $60 allowance
- Lens Enhancements: Standard progressive lenses covered at $50 copay; other enhancements at member cost
- Network: VSP Choice network

### Premium Vision Plan

- Monthly Premium: $15 (employee only), $30 (employee + spouse), $26 (employee + children), $42 (family)
- Eye Exam: $0 copay, 1 per year
- Prescription Lenses: $10 copay, 1 pair per year
- Frames Allowance: $250 per year (in-network retail), $300 at featured frame brands
- Contact Lens Allowance: $250 per year (in lieu of frames/lenses)
- Contact Lens Fitting: Fully covered (standard and specialty)
- Lens Enhancements: Progressive lenses, anti-reflective coating, photochromic lenses all covered at $0 copay
- LASIK/PRK Discount: 15% off or $100 off per eye at participating providers
- Extra Savings: 20% off additional glasses and sunglasses at in-network providers
- Network: VSP Choice and Signature networks (broader access)

## Covered Lens Types

Both plans cover the following lens types (with applicable copays):
- Single vision lenses
- Bifocal lenses
- Trifocal lenses
- Lenticular lenses
- Polycarbonate lenses (for children under 18 at no additional cost)

## Frequency of Benefits

| Benefit | Basic Plan | Premium Plan |
|---------|-----------|-------------|
| Eye Exam | Every 12 months | Every 12 months |
| Lenses | Every 12 months | Every 12 months |
| Frames | Every 24 months | Every 12 months |
| Contact Lenses | Every 12 months | Every 12 months |

## Out-of-Network Reimbursement

| Service | Basic Plan | Premium Plan |
|---------|-----------|-------------|
| Eye Exam | Up to $50 | Up to $75 |
| Single Vision Lenses | Up to $50 | Up to $75 |
| Bifocal Lenses | Up to $75 | Up to $100 |
| Progressive Lenses | Up to $75 | Up to $100 |
| Frames | Up to $70 | Up to $100 |
| Contact Lenses | Up to $105 | Up to $200 |

## Diabetic Eyecare Plus

Both plans include enhanced coverage for members diagnosed with diabetes or diabetic eye conditions. This includes additional retinal screenings and follow-up eye exams as medically necessary at no additional cost.

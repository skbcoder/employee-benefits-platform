# Open Enrollment Policy

## Annual Open Enrollment Period

Open enrollment occurs annually from November 1 through November 30. During this period, all eligible employees may make changes to their benefit elections for the upcoming plan year, which runs from January 1 through December 31.

## What Can Be Changed During Open Enrollment

During open enrollment, employees may:
- Enroll in benefits for the first time (if previously waived)
- Change medical plan tier (e.g., from Silver to Gold)
- Add or remove dependents
- Change dental or vision plan elections
- Add or drop optional life insurance coverage
- Waive coverage entirely

## Automatic Re-enrollment

Employees who do not make changes during open enrollment will be automatically re-enrolled in their current benefit elections for the upcoming plan year. Any employer contribution rates or premium changes will apply automatically.

## Late Enrollment

Enrollment submissions received after the November 30 deadline will not be processed unless accompanied by documentation of extenuating circumstances (hospitalization, natural disaster, military deployment). Late enrollment requests require HR director approval.

## Mid-Year Changes

Outside of open enrollment, benefit changes are only permitted within 30 days of a qualifying life event. See the Qualifying Life Events policy for details.

## Communication Timeline

- October 1: Open enrollment materials distributed to all eligible employees
- October 15: Benefits fair and information sessions held
- November 1: Open enrollment portal opens
- November 15: Reminder notices sent to employees who have not yet enrolled
- November 30: Open enrollment portal closes at 11:59 PM EST
- December 15: Confirmation statements mailed to all enrolled employees
- January 1: New plan year begins

## Premium Deductions

Premium deductions for the new plan year begin with the first payroll period in January. Employees should review their first January pay stub to confirm correct deduction amounts.

## Plan Year

The benefits plan year runs from January 1 through December 31, coinciding with the calendar year. All deductibles, out-of-pocket maximums, and annual limits reset on January 1.
